Not your average pub quiz

A DJ set and a trivia game, in one night

Backspin Trivia is built for bars and lounges — not boardrooms. It runs on the music your crowd actually grew up on, scored on speed so the room stays loud, with C Blak on the ones and twos between rounds. The tech keeps it smooth; the music keeps it a party.

How a night runs

01

Scan to join

Players point their phone at the QR code on screen. No app, no signup — they're in the game in seconds.

02

Answer on the beat

Each question is timed. The faster they lock in the right answer, the more points they bank — up to 17,500 a round.

03

Climb the board

The leaderboard updates live on the big screen by era and overall. The room sees who's winning — and orders another round to stay.

What's under the hood

⚡

Speed scoring

Answer fast, score big. The timing model keeps the energy high and rewards the real heads in the room.

Live era leaderboards
